Introduction

Welcome to the Javascript Adventure help page! Here, you will find information on how to use the game, its features, and how to troubleshoot any issues you may encounter.

Game Controls

Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to move your character around the game world. Press the spacebar to interact with objects or characters.

Troubleshooting

If you are experiencing any issues with the game, please follow these steps to troubleshoot the problem:

  1. Refresh the page by pressing F5 or Ctrl+R.
  2. Clear your browser's cache and refresh the page.
  3. Check your internet connection and ensure it is stable.
  4. If the issue persists, please contact the game developer for further assistance.
